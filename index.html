Werbier Hotel Luxury Ski Resort in the Swiss Alps. Perched at 1531 meters with immediate access to over 400 km of skiing and hiking terrain, Werbier mixes contemporary urban design with an alpine touch. The stylish 123 rooms and suites, each with fireplace, bed and private balcony are perfect for an intimate escape after an exhilarating day outside. Open all year-round, the hotel offers 6 restaurants and bars. Whatever guests need to amp up their stay, all they need to do is ask. Werbier team is available to make it happen 24/7.
